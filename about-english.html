🎯 Our Mission

Millions of people in India and around the world don't have access to affordable health assessments. Before spending money on expensive gym memberships or doctor visits, you can understand your basic health status right from home.

We believe every person — regardless of location or income — deserves access to quality health information to live a better life.

🔬 Science Behind Our Calculator

We use internationally recognized formulas including:

• Mifflin-St Jeor Equation for BMR calculation

• US Navy Formula for body fat estimation

• Deurenberg Formula as an alternative body fat method

• WHO Standards for BMI classification
