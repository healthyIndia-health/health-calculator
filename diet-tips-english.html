🥗 Diet Tips

Complete guide for weight loss and muscle gain through smart nutrition

🔥 Weight Loss Diet Tips

To lose weight, consume fewer calories than your TDEE. First, use our calculator to find your daily calorie needs.

💧 Stay Hydrated

Drink 8-10 glasses of water daily. Drinking a glass 30 minutes before meals naturally reduces hunger.

🕐 Meal Timing

Have breakfast by 8am, lunch at 1pm, dinner by 7pm. Avoid eating late at night — your metabolism slows after dark.

🥗 Fill Half Your Plate

Fill at least half your plate with vegetables at every meal. Low calorie, high nutrition — the perfect combination.

🍚 Choose Complex Carbs

Replace white rice and refined flour with brown rice and whole wheat. Reduce portion sizes gradually.

💪 Muscle Gain Diet Tips

Building muscle requires adequate protein and a calorie surplus above your TDEE.

🥚 Eggs Daily

Eat 3-4 eggs per day. They contain complete protein and healthy fats essential for muscle building.

🥜 Nuts & Seeds

Almonds, cashews, walnuts daily. Dense in calories, protein, and healthy fats — perfect for bulking.

🥛 Dairy Power

2-3 glasses of milk daily. Curd provides protein and calcium for bone and muscle health.

🍌 Calorie-Dense Fruits

2-3 bananas daily for carbohydrates and natural sugars that fuel muscle recovery after workouts.

📊 Protein Intake Guide

Protein is essential for both weight loss (preserving muscle) and muscle gain (building new tissue).
