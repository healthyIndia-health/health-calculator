🏃 Exercise Guide

Effective home workouts — no gym, no equipment needed

🔥 Cardio — Fat Burning Exercises

These exercises can be done at home without any equipment and are proven to burn fat effectively.

🚶

Brisk Walking

~300 Cal/hour

45 minutes of brisk walking daily is the easiest and most sustainable fat loss method.

🏃

Jogging / Running

~400-500 Cal/hour

Start with 15 minutes and gradually increase. Mix walking and jogging intervals.

⬆️

Jumping Jacks

~400 Cal/hour

3 sets × 20 reps. Full-body warm-up and calorie burner in one move.

🧘

Sun Salutation

~250 Cal/hour

10-12 rounds daily. Improves flexibility, strength, and mental focus simultaneously.

🪑

Squats

~300 Cal/hour

3 sets × 15 reps. Burns leg and belly fat while building lower body strength.

📐

Plank

~200 Cal/hour

Start with 30 seconds, build to 2 minutes. Strengthens core and improves posture.

💪 Strength Training — Muscle Building

👐

Push-Ups

Chest + Arms

3 sets × 10-15 reps. Start on knees if needed. Builds upper body strength.

🦵

Lunges

Thighs + Glutes

3 sets × 12 reps each leg. Builds balance and lower body muscle mass.

🤸

Tricep Dips

Triceps

Use a chair. 3 sets × 10 reps. Effective arm toning without weights.

🧗

Mountain Climbers

Full Body

30 seconds × 3 sets. Combines core strengthening with cardio in one exercise.

Monday

Wednesday

Thursday

Friday

Lower Body

Sunday

Let your muscles recover and rebuild
