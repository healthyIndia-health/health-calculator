📊 BMI Guide

Everything you need to know about Body Mass Index and how to improve it

📐 What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple calculation using your height and weight to assess whether you're at a healthy weight. It's one of the most widely used health screening tools worldwide.

BMI Formula

BMI = Weight (kg) ÷ Height² (m)

Example: 70kg weight, 170cm height → 70 ÷ (1.70)² = 24.2 (Normal)

⚠️ BMI Limitations

BMI is a useful screening tool but has important limitations:

• It does not distinguish between muscle and fat — a bodybuilder may have a high BMI but be very healthy.

• It does not account for age, gender, or ethnicity differences.

• Visceral fat (around organs) is more dangerous than BMI suggests.

✅ How to Improve Your BMI

1. Calorie Deficit: Reduce 500 Cal/day → lose ~0.5kg per week safely

2. Exercise: 30–45 min of walking or jogging daily burns 200–400 calories

3. Hydration: 8–10 glasses of water reduces hunger naturally

4. Sleep: 7–8 hours of quality sleep regulates hunger hormones

5. Stress Management: High cortisol from stress promotes fat storage
