🔥 Weight Loss

Body Fat Kaise Kam Kare — 10 Proven Tips

Body fat kam karna sirf diet se nahi, lifestyle change se hota hai. Yahan kuch proven tips hain jo aapko help karenge apna body fat percentage reduce karne mein.

1. Calorie Deficit banao
Apne TDEE se 300-500 calories kam khao. Zyada deficit muscle loss karta hai.
2. Protein zyada lo
Body weight ke per kg 1.6-2g protein lo. Dal, paneer, eggs, chicken best sources hain.
3. Cardio + Strength Training
Sirf cardio mat karo. Weight training se muscle bachti hai aur fat jaldi jaata hai.
4. 10,000 Steps Daily
Roz 10,000 steps chalke ~400 calories burn kar sakte ho bina gym ke.
5. 7-9 Ghante Neend lo
Kam neend se cortisol badhta hai jo belly fat store karta hai.
📊 BMI Guide

BMI Kya Hota Hai — Poori Jaankari Hindi Mein

BMI (Body Mass Index) aapke weight aur height ke ratio se calculate hota hai. Yeh batata hai ki aap healthy weight range mein hain ya nahi.

BMI < 18.5 — Underweight
Weight badhana zaroori hai. Zyada protein aur healthy calories lo.
BMI 18.5–24.9 — Normal ✅
Aap healthy range mein hain. Isko maintain karo.
BMI 25–29.9 — Overweight
Thoda weight kam karna chahiye. Diet aur exercise start karo.
BMI 30+ — Obese ⚠️
Doctor se milna chahiye. Serious lifestyle changes zaroori hain.

⚠️ Note: BMI sirf ek indicator hai. Muscular log ka BMI high ho sakta hai par wo healthy hote hain. Body fat % zyada accurate hota hai.

💪 Muscle Gain

Muscle Kaise Banaye — Beginner Guide India

Muscle gain ke liye sirf gym jaana kaafi nahi — sahi diet, neend aur consistency zaroori hai. Yeh guide especially Indians ke liye hai.

🍽️ Calorie Surplus
TDEE se 300-500 calories zyada khao. Zyada nahi warna fat badhega.
🥚 Indian Protein Sources
Eggs, dal, rajma, chole, paneer, dahi, soya chunks — sab achhe hain.
🏋️ Progressive Overload
Har hafte thoda zyada weight uthao. Isi se muscle grow karti hai.
😴 Rest & Recovery
Muscle gym mein nahi, neend mein banti hai. 7-9 ghante sona zaroori hai.
💧 Hydration

Pani Kitna Pina Chahiye — Complete Guide

Dehydration se fatigue, headache, aur weight gain ho sakta hai. Sahi hydration se metabolism fast hota hai aur skin glow karti hai.

📏 Daily Goal
2-3 litre (8-12 glasses) pani roz peeyo. Zyada exercise karo toh aur zyada.
⏰ Kab Peeye
Subah uthke 1 glass, har meal se pehle 1 glass, exercise ke baad 1-2 glass.
🚫 Avoid Karo
Cold drinks, packaged juice, chai/coffee zyada — yeh dehydrate karte hain.
🧬 Metabolism

Metabolism Kaise Badhaye — 7 Easy Ways

Slow metabolism se weight loss mushkil ho jaata hai. Yeh simple tips se apna metabolism boost karo.

1. Subah nashta zaroor karo
Breakfast skip karne se metabolism slow ho jaata hai.
2. Green Tea piyo
Roz 2-3 cup green tea metabolism ko 4-5% boost karti hai.
3. Muscle banao
Muscle tissue fat se zyada calories burn karta hai, even at rest.
4. Thanda pani piyo
Body thanda pani warm karne mein extra calories burn karti hai.
5. Stress kam karo
High cortisol se metabolism slow aur belly fat badh jaata hai.
